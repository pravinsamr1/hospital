About Dr. V.K. Sharma

I am Dr. V.K. Sharma, a retired Director from the Sports Authority of India. For the past twenty years, I have dedicated myself to developing a rehabilitation program centered around musculoskeletal biomechanics, aimed at preventing and reversing lifestyle-related diseases and disorders. Through extensive research on various training approaches, I have crafted a robust and scientifically-backed training method that integrates interactive breathing with movement science, creating a comprehensive framework to address musculoskeletal and physiological imbalances resulting from modern lifestyles.

This approach is based on the inherent structural and functional design of the human body, where fundamental movements around each joint are practiced using the body’s weight while coordinating with breathing in a lying position. This method enhances mobility, strength, and endurance, promoting longevity in our musculoskeletal system and allowing for everyday activities to be performed with greater ease and comfort.
